Accessibility is part of 'done'

Accessibility is part of being 'done', and it is the manager's responsibility to ensure that has been accounted for. In most cases it is a matter of ensuring that the team are checking the work as they go along.

How to include accessibility as part of done

For a simple statement about "done", you can use these four main questions:

  • Can you use it without a mouse?
  • Does it provide good contrast and enable zooming?
  • Does it provide appropriate information to screen readers?
  • It is easy to understand?

If in doubt: ask questions early.

The sooner the accessibility requirements are considered, the easier it is.

The amount of testing & validation towards the end of a project will vary depending on the scale of the project and type of changes that are being made. If it is a new service, or replacement for a service then you should arrange 3rd party testing. If it is a small update, the teams internal testing may be sufficient.
