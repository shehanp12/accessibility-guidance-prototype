Do not rely on colour or position

Content

People who can't see may not understand instructions that refer to colour or the location of page elements.

For example, do not say "Press the red button on the right".

If someone is:

  • zoomed in, the button may not be on the right
  • "colour blind", they may not be able to tell the difference between red and green
  • using a screenreader, they may not see the colour - and location, for them, is simply up or down
