Time to read and act

Testing

There should not be a time limit for inactivity that is less than 20 hours. For example, time limiting a purchase or multi-step form.

For 3rd party content that does use a time-limit, a clear warning should appear to notify all users on the time they have remaining, users should also be able to:

  • Extend the time limit, or
  • Turn off the time limit

There are exceptions for when the time limit is essential to the activity (e.g. an online test) or part of a real time event (e.g. an auction).
