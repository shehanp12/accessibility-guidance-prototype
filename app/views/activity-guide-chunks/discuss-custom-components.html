Discuss any custom components

For: Design, Frontend

The components in the service manual are well tested and ready to use.

If you need a new component, discuss it with other members of your team. You need to make sure that:

  • you have good evidence that it's the best way of meeting the user need
  • it will be accessible
  • you test it

Bear in mind that new components add an overhead of testing, both from a technical and a usability point of view.
