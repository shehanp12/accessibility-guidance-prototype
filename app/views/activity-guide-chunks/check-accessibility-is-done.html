Check that accessibility is 'done'

Product and delivery

It's the product or delivery manager's responsibility to make sure that that the product or service is accessible. In most cases it is a matter of making sure that the team is checking the work as they go along.

If in doubt: ask questions early. The sooner you consider accessibility requirements, the easier it is.

4 simple questions to check it's 'done'

For a simple check that it's 'done', ask:

  • Can you use it without a mouse?
  • Does it provide good contrast and allow zooming?
  • Is it easy to understand?
  • Does it give screen readers appropriate information?
