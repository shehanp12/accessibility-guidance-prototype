Flashes and animation

Testing

Pause, Stop, Hide

Examine the page to identify any content that moves, blinks and scrolls automatically and also lasts more than 5 seconds, where such content is implemented ensure that a functionality is provided which gives the ability to pause, hide or stop the content.

Three Flashes or Below Threshold

Examine the page to identify content that flashes more than 3 times in any 1 second, or the flash is below the general flash and red flash thresholds.
