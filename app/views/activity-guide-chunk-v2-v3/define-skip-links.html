Define skip links

Design

Where there are many links or form controls are used in one group, it creates a lot of items to tab through. A link at the start of the group to skip to the end of the links helps people using keyboard-style access with a switch or head-wand to avoid repetitive key-presses.

Skip links can be invisible (off screen) by default, but must be very visible when focused.
